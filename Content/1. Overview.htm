1. Overview

This guide is intended for technical teams who will build Google Dialogflow CX virtual agents. The document provides an overview of relevant concepts, platform components, and configuration instructions that you need to create and manage production quality conversational flow.

Dialogflow CX is enterprise-grade conversational AI platform for building virtual agents that understand natural language, enable automated workflows between users and backend systems and scale both from small to truly complex interfaces.
